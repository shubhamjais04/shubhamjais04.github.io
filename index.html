Who Am I?

I'm Shubham Jaiswal, a Data Scientist & ML Engineer currently interning at Amdox Technologies, and finishing my B.Sc. in Statistics & Computer Science from Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith (MGKVP), Varanasi — graduating May 2026.

My journey into data science began with a simple curiosity: how can machines learn patterns from data and make intelligent decisions? That question led me deep into Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Statistical Modeling, and production AI deployment — and I haven't stopped building since.

What sets me apart is that I don't just experiment in notebooks — I ship real, working applications. I've worked on medical diagnosis AI, financial fraud detection, loan default risk analytics, and retail sales intelligence — all deployed end-to-end. I believe in clean code, measurable results, and engineering that actually works in the real world.

Outside of building projects, I'm constantly learning — through certifications, self-directed learning, and active Kaggle participation.. I'm actively looking for a full-time role where I can work on challenging, real-world problems alongside a team that values solid engineering and clear thinking.
